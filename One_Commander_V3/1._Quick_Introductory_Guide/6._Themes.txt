====== 6. Themes ======

One Commander allows you to change themes and create new ones. Initially two themes are provided: the default, and DarkAcrylic. You can switch between these themes by <instructions for switching themes>.

Detailed information about changing these themes or adding new ones is [[2. Full Reference Guide:Themes|here]].
